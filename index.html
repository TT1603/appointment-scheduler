Set Priority:

1) Set priority of appointments by cliking one of the three buttons. Default priority is "Normal".

2) Fill in forms to set title, hour, and detail.

3) Title must not be empty, and hour must be an integer from 0 to 23.

4) Click "Add appointment" to add entries to the schedule. Schedule will be shown upon addition.

5) Click on each entry in the schedule to view details. Click on a second time to clear this view.

6) Click "Remove" to remove any entry.

7) Click "Clear all" to clear the schedule and all text fields.
